Quarphix Tracking App

The Quarphix Learner Tracking Project is aimed at developing a comprehensive system that will track the status of all Quarphix learners from their onboarding to deployment or offboarding stages. This system will also track the progress of interns through their training stages. The system will be built on the Salesforce platform and will be accessible to various departments, including the Administrator, Sales Personnel, HR, and Training Administrator. It will serve as a central hub for managing both learner and consultant profiles, project assignments, client contracts, and intern training progress.

Real-Time Face Mask Detection Using YOLOv8

This Face Mask Detection system is a real-time AI-powered solution designed to ensure public health safety by identifying individuals wearing or not wearing masks. Built using YOLOv8, the model delivers fast and accurate mask detection, making it ideal for use in public spaces, workplaces, and healthcare environments.

Key Features & Benefits:

Real-Time Detection – Instantly identifies people with or without masks High Accuracy – Powered by YOLOv8 for precise recognition Efficient & Lightweight – Runs on various devices, from PCs to edge AI devices Improves Public Safety – Helps enforce mask policies in high-risk areas Customizable – Can be trained for different environments and mask types Automated Alerts – Can be integrated with security systems for real-time notifications This project enhances safety measures and contributes to the fight against COVID-19 by promoting responsible mask usage in public settings.

Online Voting App: Secure & Convenient Digital Voting
